Website Evaluations

Apple

Apple’s official site is clean, minimal, and highly user-friendly.

  • Contrast: Excellent use of white space and bold images.
  • Repetition: Consistent fonts, colors, and layout unify the site.
  • Alignment: Content follows a clear grid structure and it looks organized.
  • Proximity: Related content is grouped logically.

Ling’s Cars

Ling’s Cars is a little bit of a mess.

  • Contrast: Too many clashing colors make the site overwhelming.
  • Repetition: No consistent design pattern.
  • Alignment: Elements feel scattered with little structure.
  • Proximity: Related and unrelated elements are jumbled together.
