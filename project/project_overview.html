Project Overview – División Urbana Website

Project Overview

The purpose of this project is to design and develop a website for División Urbana, a Nicaraguan-American rock band led by Orlando Marco. The website will serve as an online hub for the band’s fans and new listeners, showcasing their music, photos, and information.

The goal of the site is to increase visibility, connect with fans, and promote the band’s work through an organized and visually appealing platform.

Intended Users

  • Fans of División Urbana and local rock music
  • New listeners discovering the band online

Overview of Content

  • Home Page: Introduction to the band and latest news or announcements.
  • About Page: History of División Urbana, band members, and musical influences.
  • Music Page: Embedded audio and video players for songs and music videos.
  • Gallery Page: Photo gallery from concerts and recording sessions.
  • Contact Page: Contact form and links to social media accounts.
